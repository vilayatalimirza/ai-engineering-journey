7-Day Kolkata Itinerary: Food, Shopping & Walking
Kolkata is best experienced on foot, where the "City of Joy" reveals its history through its street food and colonial architecture.

Day 1: The Colonial Heart (Dalhousie Square)

Morning: A heritage walk around B.B.D. Bagh (Dalhousie Square). See the Writers' Building, GPO, and St. John’s Church.
Lunch: Authentic Bengali thali at Kewpie’s or 6 Ballygunge Place.
Evening: Walk along the Hooghly River at Prinsep Ghat for sunset.
Day 2: Markets & Old School Charm

Morning: Visit the Mullik Ghat Flower Market (very early) for incredible photos and a sensory explosion.
Shopping: New Market (Hogg Market). It’s chaotic but essential for everything from silver jewelry to cheese.
Food: Try the famous mutton cutlets at Chitto Babur Dokan in Dacres Lane.
